Welcome to Nanital

Located in the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand, Nainital is a lakeside hill station known for its beautiful landscape. ...Verdant mountains lace the emerald green Naini Lake, further making way for offbeat trails, lakes, and hilltops alike. Experiences like boating, hiking, and stunning views of snow-clad Himalayas primarily bring travellers to Nainital. However, one can also explore nearby villages and sites to indulge in paragliding, rappelling, camping, or purely just unwinding amidst Nature. The finest rejuvenating destinations around Nainital include Mukteshwar, Ramgarh, Naukuchiatal, Pangot, Ranikhet, and Jeolikot.
