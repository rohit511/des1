Welcome to Jaisalmer

Jaisalmer is golden in the most profound sense, with the colour reflecting in its houses, streets, and desert alike. Travellers look forward to a lip-smacking plate of daal baati, walking through a fort which is a city in itself, and exploring the textiles to bring home a skirt or a mat. Accompanying these, Jaisalmer gives you a surreal experience of camping in the desert, watching the stars swoosh over your head and meeting the sun as it rises over the dunes.

Why we love jaisalmer

...Right on the outskirts of Jaislmer, as you pass through the colourful villages, the golden desert awaits you. Spend a night on a desert Safari amidst shimmering sand dunes with Zostel and you’ll see what the hype is about. One thing that Jaisalmer can never fall short on is the royalty. The city offers you a rich experience of living in an actual 150-year-old fort, Zostel of course being a part of it! Riveting views of the city, golden-coloured houses in narrow lanes and the glistening desert- pretty much sums up what the fort life of Jaisalmer is like. Every Indian festival is celebrated here with togetherness. Be it the locals or travellers, all are welcome at the fort to participate in the festivities. And if you happen to celebrate Holi here, you’re lucky!
