Welcome to Delhi

A little goes a long way when it comes to exploring Delhi and one thing that’s for sure is that you won’t have to travel too far to explore its absolute best. The narrow lanes of Old Delhi overflooded with people tantalise you with the most palatable street food, whereas the quiet streets of Central & Southern Delhi house monuments that speak of the royal Mughal dynasty galore. With a kickass nightlife, impressive food scene, and an arena of places to explore, shop, and live in, Delhi is exemplary of big-city life with its pleasures and luxuries alike.

Why we love Delhi

...The Khan Market is a shopping street popular with elite Indians and expats in Central Delhi. It is the 21st most expensive retail high street in the whole world and India’s most expensive market. Displaying an array of chic eat-outs (such as Civil House and Town Hall) and luxury indie brands for shopping (such as Anokhi and Amrapali) this cobblestoned market looks a lot like an Indian style European market.
